From the editors

The default looks of our AI tools were never designed for the way we actually use them — for hours at a time, late into the evening, with our attention nowhere else.

So we made Vellum. Twelve themes free, forever. Thirty-two more for the people who want them. Every one tuned by hand and tested in real conversations until it felt right.

It is a small thing. We hope it helps.

Atmosphere, not noise.

Animations are slow, careful, and never the focus. They sit in the corners of your attention, the way a good room makes you feel without you noticing why. Less than 1% CPU on a typical day.

Three short notes

Built with restraint, where most extensions reach for spectacle.

N°. 01

Privacy by absence.

No tracking, no analytics, no telemetry. Vellum runs entirely in your browser. Your theme syncs through your Chrome account if you're signed in, and that's the extent of it.

N°. 02

Both platforms, one home.

Whether you live in Claude.ai or ChatGPT — or move between them daily — Vellum reskins both seamlessly. Your selection is remembered everywhere.

N°. 03

Updates, included.

New themes are added monthly. Pro members receive every release without any extra charge. We never paywall existing themes after release. Promise.
